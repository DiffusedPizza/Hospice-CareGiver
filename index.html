Certifications

  • Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation

    CPR or Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation is an emergency lifesaving procedure performed when the heart stops beating. Immediate CPR can double or triple chances of survival after cardiac arrest.

  • First Aid

    First aid can include cleaning minor cuts, scrapes, or scratches; treating a minor burn; applying bandages and dressings; the use of non-prescription medicine; and removing debris from the eyes.

    The fingerprint clearance card plays a crucial role in safeguarding public safety and upholding professional integrity by preventing those with a criminal history from working in sensitive areas.
